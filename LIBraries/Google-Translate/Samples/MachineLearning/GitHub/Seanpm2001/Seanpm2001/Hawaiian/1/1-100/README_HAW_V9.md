## Kūlana polokalamu

Pau ʻole kaʻu mau hana i kekahi mau kapu. DRM (** D ** igital ** R ** estrications ** M ** anagement) ʻaʻole i loaʻa i kekahi o kaʻu mau hana.

! [DRM-free_label.en.svg] (DRM-free_label.en.svg)

Kākoʻo ʻia kēia pepili e ka Free Software Foundation. ʻAʻole wau i manaʻo e hoʻokomo iā DRM i kaʻu mau hana.

Ke hoʻohana nei au i ka pōkole ʻo "Digital Restrictions Management" ma kahi o ka "Digital Rights Management" i ʻike nui ʻia e like me ke ʻano maʻamau o ka haʻi ʻana iā ia he wahaheʻe, ʻaʻohe kuleana me DRM. ʻOi aku ka pololei o ka pela ʻana o ka pela ʻana, a kākoʻo ʻia e [Richard M. Stallman (RMS)] (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Stallman) a me ka [Free Software Foundation (FSF)] ( https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_Software_Foundation)

Hoʻohana ʻia kēia ʻāpana e hāpai i ka ʻike no nā pilikia me DRM, a e kūʻē hoʻi iā ia. Hemahema ʻo DRM e ka hoʻolālā a he mea hoʻoweliweli nui i nā mea hoʻohana kamepiula āpau a me ke kūʻokoʻa o ka polokalamu.

ʻAoʻao kiʻi: [defectivebydesign.org/drm-free/...aina(https://www.defectivebydesign.org/drm-free/how-to-use-label)

### Mōʻaukala buff

ʻO wau kahi punahele mōʻaukala, me ka ʻike o ka mōʻaukala e ʻike nei ma hope o 13.8 biliona mau makahiki i hala (Big Bang) a hiki i kēia lā. Makemake wau e aʻo e pili ana i ka mōʻaukala, a ʻike wau i kahi ʻano nui o ka wā a me nā hanana, me ka ʻike nui o nā mea ʻē aʻe ma mua o ke Kaua Honua Honua a i ʻole ke Kaua Honua II (akā ʻike nui nō hoʻi wau e pili ana i nā kaua honua 2) no ka Honua moʻolelo, hoʻi kaʻu ʻike i 8000 BCE i nā kikoʻī kikoʻī, a hiki i 27000 BCE i ka liʻiliʻi kikoʻī, a me ka hapanui o nā hanana kālaihonua ma mua o 27000 TK Makemake au e aʻo i nā ʻano mōʻaukala āpau, ʻaʻole wale i ka mōʻaukala o ke kaua.

ʻAi nui wau i koʻu hoihoi i ka mōʻaukala i kaʻu kumu mōʻaukala High School mua loa, nāna i hoihoi nui e pili ana i kāna mea e aʻo mai nei, a he kumu moʻolelo mōʻaukala maikaʻi a maikaʻi hoʻi. ʻAʻole wau e kanalua e hoʻolilo iā ia i kumu o ka makahiki no kēlā makahiki o ke kula kiʻekiʻe. ʻAʻole nā ​​papa o ka mōʻaukala i hala iaʻu i loko o ke kumuhana o ka mōʻaukala, akā ua wehe maoli kēia i koʻu noʻonoʻo i ka mōʻaukala.
